Mission FO Candy (Individual Story)
Mission FO Candy is a story about a witch-human scientist named Eleanor attempting to make a familiar despite her awfully weak magic strength until unexpectedly one of her attempts ends up being a success despite this lack of magical strength. Though the familiar displayed unusual behaviour. Instead of being obedient, the familiar is hyperactive and candy obsessed so Eleanor goes on a quest for research, lying to her new companion that it’s a “mission for candy.”

Welcome to the Solar System! (Project)
Welcome to the Solar System is a project about personified celestial objects in our solar system. The solar system is this kingdom that the Sun created, divided into 5 distinct regions overseen by the planets of each area. The Core is overseen by the Sun and Mercury, the Inner Town is overseen by Venus, Earth, and Mars, the Asteroid Belt is overseen by Jupiter, and the Main Town is overseen by Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune, and Neptune also oversees the Kuiper Belt.
